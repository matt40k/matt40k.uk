One of the problems with traditional HDD is the fact they have moving parts, if your moving your laptop around and you bang it, it can get damage, Toshiba have implemented a safely feature where when movement is detected it moved the HDD file header to a safe position. It make sound a little bit extreme, but when your talking about a disc that spins at over 4000 RPM and is wafer thin, you kinda get why they&#8217;ve add the feature. Personally, SSD is a better fix.
